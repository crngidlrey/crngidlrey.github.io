Welcome to Conanian Nations

Welcome to Conanian Nations! Conanian Nations is a community dedicated to all fans of Detective Conan across the globe. Here, we celebrate the adventures of Shinichi Kudo, also known as Conan Edogawa, as he solves mysteries and brings justice.

Plot

Shinichi Kudo, a great mystery expert at only seventeen, is already well known for having solved several challenging cases. One day, when Shinichi sees two suspicious men and decides to follow them, he inadvertently becomes witness to a disturbing illegal activity...
